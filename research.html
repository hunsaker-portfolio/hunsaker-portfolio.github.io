Research Interests

I have been fortunate to gain research experience as an undergraduate research assistant on a project that overlaps with my primary areas of interests. As I continue pursuing my own research and apply to graduate programs, these will be my primary areas of study.

My research interests are:

  • Political Philosophy
  • Political Theory
  • Political Economy
  • American Studies

Political philosophy and theory are two areas of focus where I hope to concentrate my work. In my undergraduate studies I complete a concentration in Law, Philosophy, and Governance, and also assisted on a project where historical instances were theorized as examples of political phenomena.

Political economy and American studies are two other research areas where I am interested. I believe the American political ecosystem is a unique one, and its tendencies might be effectively analyzed through an economic lens. Examples of my previous research, including a political psychology research paper, are available in Work Samples.
