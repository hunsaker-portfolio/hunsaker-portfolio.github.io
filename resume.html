I believe I have a diverse array of previous experiences that make me a unique candidate for administrative and academic work. In my capacity as a research assistant I performed a number of different functions: assembling annotated bibliographies, composing memos, finding archival data and images, as well as proofreading and assisting with manuscript preparation. The project I assisted on is Democracy in Captivity, a study of democratic experiments in incarcerated environments. The convergence of themes and diversity of tasks allowed me to hone my skills as a research assistant.
